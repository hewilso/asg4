Diamond Mining

Please use a browser that supports "canvas"





Yesterday you got onto the metro through the backdoor instead of the front. Today you woke up in this cell. You've been told that if you mine 15 diamond blocks you can escape. Although you doubt you will ever be granted true freedom, you might as well try mining. Move around with the WASD keys. A diamond block can be placed in front of you with the R key, and you can mine with the F key. The counter goes up everytime you mine a block.
